# XXOON Motion System

## Motion Philosophy

Movement creates emotional energy.

Dance, sports motion, crowd behavior, rhythm, celebration, and environmental activity should make the worlds feel alive.

## Motion Types

- Dancehall movement
- Carnival movement
- Sports action
- Character behavior
- Crowd energy
- Environmental motion
- Performance animation

## Emotional Motion

Motion should reflect:
- excitement
- tension
- celebration
- fear
- confidence
- mystery
- atmosphere

## Living Environment

Background movement and world activity should continuously reinforce the feeling that the ecosystem is alive.
